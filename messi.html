Lionel Messi

Introduction

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in football history.

Personal Information
  • Full Name: Lionel Andrés Messi
  • Birthdate: June 24, 1987
  • Nationality: Argentine
  • Position: Forward

Career Highlights
  1. FC Barcelona (2004–2021)
  2. Paris Saint-Germain (2021–2023)
  3. Inter Miami (2023–Present)

Achievements
  • 8× Ballon d'Or winner
  • FIFA World Cup winner (2022)
  • Most goals for a single club (Barcelona)
  • Over 800 career goals

Quote
"You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it." – Lionel Messi
