Zlatan Ibrahimović

Introduction

Zlatan Ibrahimović is a Swedish former professional footballer known for his confidence, strength, and incredible goals. He played as a striker.

Personal Information

  • Birthdate: October 3, 1981
  • Nationality: Swedish
  • Position: Striker

Achievements
