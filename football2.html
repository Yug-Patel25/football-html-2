🌍 What is Football?

Football (also called soccer) is the most loved and watched sport globally. It is played by two teams of eleven players who aim to score goals by kicking a ball into the opponent's net.

"Some people believe football is a matter of life and death... I assure you, it's much more serious than that." – Bill Shankly

📜 A Brief History

Football dates back to the mid-19th century in England. Since then, it has evolved into the modern-day global sport governed by FIFA.

📖 Basic Rules

  • Each match lasts 90 minutes (two halves of 45 minutes).
  • No hands allowed (except the goalkeeper inside the penalty box).
  • Team with the most goals wins.

🏆 Famous Tournaments

  1. FIFA World Cup
  2. UEFA Champions League
  3. Copa América
  4. English Premier League
