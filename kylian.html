Kylian Mbappé

Introduction

Kylian Mbappé is a French footballer known for his pace, skill, and goal-scoring abilities. He plays as a forward for club and country.

Personal Information
  • Full Name: Kylian Mbappé Lottin
  • Birthdate: December 20, 1998
  • Nationality: French
  • Position: Forward

Career Highlights
  1. AS Monaco (2015–2017)
  2. Paris Saint-Germain (2017–2024)
  3. Real Madrid (2024–Present)

Achievements
  • FIFA World Cup winner (2018)
  • Golden Boot (2022 World Cup)
  • Multiple Ligue 1 titles
  • Top scorer in PSG history

Quote
"I always assume that one day someone will come and try to do better so you always have to go beyond your limits." – Kylian Mbappé
