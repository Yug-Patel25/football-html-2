Cristiano Ronaldo

Introduction

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He is widely considered one of the greatest football players of all time.

Personal Information
  • Full Name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
  • Birthdate: February 5, 1985
  • Nationality: Portuguese
  • Position: Forward

Career Highlights
  1. Sporting CP (2002–2003)
  2. Manchester United (2003–2009, 2021–2022)
  3. Real Madrid (2009–2018)
  4. Juventus (2018–2021)
  5. Al-Nassr (2023–Present)

Achievements
  • 5× Ballon d'Or winner
  • UEFA Champions League winner (5 times)
  • Over 850 career goals
  • Most international goals in men's football history

Quotes
"Your love makes me strong, your hate makes me unstoppable." – Cristiano Ronaldo
